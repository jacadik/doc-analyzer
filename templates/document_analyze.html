Analysis Guide
Understanding the Analysis
  • Duplicate Paragraphs - Identical paragraphs that appear multiple times within this document.
  • Similar Paragraphs - Paragraphs that are not identical but have similar content above the threshold.
  • Common Phrases - Recurring text fragments that appear throughout the document.
Tips
  • Lower the threshold to find more similar paragraphs with minor differences.
  • Look for patterns in the similar content to identify template structures.
  • Pay attention to common phrases that might indicate standardized language.
